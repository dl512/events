Frequently Asked Questions

Who are we?

Think of it as a TED Talk-like forum where anyone—regardless of background or experience—can share their passions. We believe that when these passions are crafted into thoughtful speeches, they have the power to inspire everyone in attendance.

I am not a professor or a Nobel Prize winner; can I join as a speaker?

Absolutely! If you have a topic you're passionate about, we want to hear from you.

I hate sitting in class. Is this not for me?

We believe curiosity is seeded in everyone's mind! We cover all sorts of topics, and we're sure something will resonate with you.

How long is each session?

Theoretically, each session lasts two hours. This may include 1-3 presentations from our speakers, followed by a Q&A and opportunities to connect with others. However, in reality, people often stay behind and chat for a lot longer.
